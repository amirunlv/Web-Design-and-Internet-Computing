Parental Influence on Decision Making

Hello world! Today's topic is the parental influence on decision making.

Coming from a South-Asian household, parental pressure is everything. Even though they make comparable incomes, desi parents think of computer programmers as inferior compared to doctors. Adolescents growing up with such a mindset surrounding them can be easily swayed from what better fits their personal interests. Personally, I believe that life is too short to have someone else's opinions impact decisions you make that will affect your entire timeline.
